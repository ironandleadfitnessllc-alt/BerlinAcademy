🌞 Monday Routine

Wake up
Make bed
Feed animals
Take shower
Eat breakfast
Brush hair
Brush teeth
Clean up bathroom
Clean up kitchen
Clean up table

🧘 7:00 AM – Mind & Body

15 min Yoga
10 min Meditation
Watch 1 video on time management, productivity, or goal setting
